The Ecosystem of ITEMS on top of ETHEREUM

ETHITEM is a new way to buidl interoperable objects on top of Ethereum, basically is an ERC20, ERC1155, ERC721 in a single item, unleashing the true power of dapps, games, art and DeFi.

Use it EVERYWHERE

ETHITEMS are interoperable programmable portable dapps that can be used everywhere, immagine preparing yourself for the next Raid in your favorite ETH based videogame, by buying the potions and weapons needed in Uniswap or even borrow other in game items by earning DAI, or even farm Cryptovoxels items.

BUIDL Portable Dapps

Every Item is builded by extension contracts, so you can build complex technology behind them, making ethItems the first general purpose interoperable and programmable Token Standard. Using ethItems, you can finally call all of the existing ethereum item via the proxy contracts, without needing to hardcode every address.

ITEM the Programmable Equity of the ETHITEM DFO

ETHITEM protocol is builded on top of a Decentralized Flexible Organization, totally ruled on-chain without any external owners of the code and it's updates. The ITEM token is the programmable equity of it and nobody except ITEM holders can rule it
